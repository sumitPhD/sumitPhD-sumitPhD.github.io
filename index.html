    Brief history of me

    Hi! Welcome to my Homepage.
    I am a research scholar in Mechanical Engineering Department at IIT,Bombay. My hometown is Raipur, Chhattisgarh. Currently I am working on gyroscope sensor developement, a device which is used for measuring angular rate, under the guidance of Prof. V. Kartik. In parallel I am also contributing in development of high speed AFM for imaging of biological samples.

    I am a member of Attitude Determination and Control Subsystem of Student Satellite Club, IITB. Recently we have launched wiki to share the knowledge, the team has gathererd over the years and to promote space activities in India.

    My hobbies are playing tennis, skating, reading novels, watching movies and fundamental trading. I completed 42km Standard Chartered Mumbai Marathon in Jan 2017. My favourite novel is 'The Book Thief' and I am a die hard fan of 'Fight Club' movie. Oops! I should not have talked about it. Although I do not watch much cricket, my role model is Rahul Dravid.

    I am instrested in solving problem related to vibration, dynamics and control. I also enjoy learning electronics stuff, android app development and embedded system.
